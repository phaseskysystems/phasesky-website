About PhaseSky

Our mission and principles

Mission Statement

PhaseSky designs and builds synchronized micro‑drone systems, advancing distributed aerial intelligence through physics‑driven engineering and disciplined innovation.

We exist to engineer aerial systems where signal, timing, and flight converge. Our work documents real constraints, failures, and progress—without exaggeration or hype.

Values

  • Future‑Forward Engineering — we design with the next decade in mind.
  • Aerospace Discipline — precision and rigor guide every decision.
  • Distributed Intelligence — we build systems that work together seamlessly.
  • Physics‑Driven Design — we respect the laws that govern flight.
  • Minimalism with Intent — form serves function, nothing more.
  • Credibility & Seriousness — no hype, only results.

Era & Inspiration

PhaseSky operates at the dawn of the distributed intelligence age (2025–2040). Our inspiration comes from modern aerospace, early spaceflight programs, and the disciplined engineering of the Cold War era.
